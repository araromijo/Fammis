Welcome to Fammis Place

Located in the heart of Badagry, Lagos, Fammis Place is your premier destination for luxury cakes, artisan pastries, and premium ice cream. Our passion for baking excellence and use of the finest ingredients has made us the go-to spot for special occasions and everyday indulgence.

From our signature luxury cakes adorned with African-inspired designs to our perfectly flaky meat pies and refreshing ice cream, every creation is crafted with love, precision, and the finest ingredients.
